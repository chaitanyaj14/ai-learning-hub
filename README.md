## üß≠ What This Roadmap Covers

The roadmap progresses in **phases**, from fundamentals to cutting-edge systems:

1. **Foundations**
   - What is AI, ML, Deep Learning
   - Math & intuition (no heavy equations)
   - Data, features, and evaluation basics

2. **Machine Learning Core**
   - Supervised & Unsupervised Learning
   - Classical ML algorithms
   - Model training and evaluation

3. **Deep Learning**
   - Neural networks
   - CNNs, RNNs
   - Optimization and regularization

4. **Transformers & LLMs**
   - Attention & Transformers
   - Large Language Models
   - Prompting fundamentals

5. **Generative AI**
   - Text, image, audio generation
   - Diffusion models
   - Multimodal models

6. **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**
   - Embeddings
   - Vector databases
   - Production RAG patterns

7. **Agentic AI**
   - AI agents & tool use
   - Planning & reasoning
   - Multi-agent systems

8. **Production & Real-World AI**
   - Evaluation & monitoring
   - Safety & alignment
   - Responsible AI
